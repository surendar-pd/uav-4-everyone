DrIFT: Autonomous **Dr**one Dataset with **I**ntegrated Real and Synthetic Data, **F**lexible Views, and **T**ransformed Domains. This dataset addresses drone detection challenges under domain shifts such as background, point of view, weather, and real-to-synthetic transitions.
